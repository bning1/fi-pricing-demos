Funding Value Adjustment (FVA) - MC Demo

Simplified MC for unilateral FVA on swap-like forward. GBM risk factor X(t), MTM(t) = Notional × (X(t)-1), EE(t) = max(0, MTM(t)). Integrand(t) = EE(t) × spread × DF(t,T), ΔFVA(t) = Integrand(t) × Δt, CumFVA(t) = ∑ ΔFVA. Daily steps (252/yr). Constant funding spread & r. No collateral/netting/thresholds. 95% quantile curves.
