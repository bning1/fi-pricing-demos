Liquidity Valuation Adjustment (LVA) vs FVA - Uncollateralized MC Demo

Simplified Monte Carlo for FVA (full tenor) & LVA (liq horizon H). Risk factor X(t) ~ GBM(μ, σ), MTM(t) = Notional × (X(t) - 1), EE(t) = max(0, MTM(t)). FVA = ∫_0^T EE(t) × funding_spread × DF(t) dt. LVA = ∫_0^H EE(t) × liq_spread × DF(t) dt (trapezoidal). Daily steps (252/yr).
