Debt Valuation Adjustment (DVA) - Bilateral Net CVA/DVA MC Demo

Simplified Monte Carlo bilateral XVA. Risk factor X(t) ~ GBM(μ, σ), MTM(t) = Notional × (X(t) - 1).
Counterparty EE(t)=max(0, MTM(t)), Own EE(t)=max(0, -MTM(t)).
CVA ≈ ∑ PFE_CP(t) × λ_CP × (1-R_CP) × DF(t) × Δt
DVA ≈ ∑ PFE_own(t) × λ_own × (1-R_own) × DF(t) × Δt
Net Adj = -CVA + DVA (liability benefit if own λ high).
Daily steps (252/yr), flat λ/r. No collateral/netting.
