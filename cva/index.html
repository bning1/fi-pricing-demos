Credit Valuation Adjustment (CVA) - CC Risk MC Demo

Simplified Monte Carlo for CVA from swap-like forward exposures. Risk factor X(t) ~ GBM(μ, σ), MTM(t) = Notional × (X(t) - 1), EE(t) = max(0, MTM(t)). PFE(t) = quantile(EE(t)). CVA ≈ ∑ PFE(t) × λ × LGD × DF(t) × Δt (daily steps, 252/yr). Flat λ/r. No collateral/netting.
